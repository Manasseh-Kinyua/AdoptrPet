WHY GET YOUR PET?

Adopted pets have just as much love to give.


You save a life.
Animals at our shelter are in need of a second chance. They have been lost, given up or abandoned. You are giving them a new life in a loving home.


Adult pets are great!
Often they are already housetrained and some can even “sit” or “stay.” You will also be able to see the personality of the adult animal without having to wait.

Low adoption fees.
Pet adoption fees are much less than the cost of purebred puppies or kittens sold for profit.

You will change a homeless animal's whole world. And get a new furry best friend out of the deal.

Access to support and resources
Our vets will examine the pets at no extra charge for a duration of 12 months after adoption as a part of the pet adoption.
